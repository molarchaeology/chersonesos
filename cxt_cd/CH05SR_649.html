Description

  • CH05SR0649
  • Compact light brown layer mixed with stones in SE part of room 38
  • Compact light brown layer mixed with loose stones, pottery and bones. It is cut (650) along the western cut of the bedrock (728)
  • The layer was rich of pottery and we have recorded an higher number of special finds than the trampling surface 610. So it could be that it was an earlier trampling surface.
