Interpretation

    • Ashy layer associated with metalworking activities across Area 3 of the coutyard. This context may have been the final use phase of the courtyard and though the fine ashy soil is from metalworking activity, the surface and relatively thin depth is the result of light use (ie trampling), possibly after metalworking had ceased.
