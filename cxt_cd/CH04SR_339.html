Description

  • CH04SR0339
  • collapse in room 29
  • Layer of medium to large stones in matrix of brown earth; covers entire area of room 29.
  • Possible that the cut/disturbance in SR343 also cut this layer but that it was not noticed in the upper levels. So the chronological relationship between this layer and SR343 is not quite certain. May equal SR 338 -- NB from 2005 revision: according to interpretation of stratigraphic sequence, the two pits found lower in this layer were almost certainly cut into its surface and not noticed during excavation (since no separate layer recognizable in which the cuts could have begun); as a result, stratigraphic relationships edited on 8 July 2005 to reflect two cuts in 339
